⭐ Required — move Waypoint above Cinemeta

Stremio shows the details from whichever addon is highest in your list. To make Waypoint's resume hint appear, it has to sit above Cinemeta:

  1. Open the Stremio Addon Manager ↗ and sign in.
  2. Drag Waypoint near the top of the list — above Cinemeta.
  3. Click Sync to Stremio.

Tip: re-syncing here also refreshes Stremio's cache if a hint ever looks stale.
